#  Records (History) Screen Specifications

##  Layout & Colors
- **Background:** `0xFFFFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Time Filter Tabs:** - Active (Week): White with Black Border.
  - Inactive (Month/Year): Dark Grey/Black background.
- **Empty State Text:** `0xFF000000` (Black for "No records yet") and Grey for the subtitle.
- **Floating Action Button:** `0xFF00C853` (Success Green) with White "+".
- **Navigation Bar:** `0xFF1A1C1E` (Dark Charcoal).
- **Active Icon (Records):** `0xFF00C853` (Green).

##  Layout Details
- **Header:** "Records" text centered at the top.
- **Filter Bar:** Horizontal row with three equal segments (Week, Month, Year). 
- **Sub-Filter:** Black bar showing "Week 9", "Week 10", etc., with white text.
- **Empty State:** Large centered "Note/Receipt" icon (`ic_no_transactions.svg`) with the text "No records yet" and "Your expenses will appear here" below it.
- **Floating Action Button:** Green circle placed at the bottom right, just above the navigation bar.
- **Navigation Bar:** Fixed at the bottom with three sections (Home, Charts, Records).
