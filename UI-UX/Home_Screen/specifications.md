#  Home Screen

##  Layout & Colors
- **Background:** `0xFFFFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Top Balance Card:** `0xFF1A1C1E` (Dark Charcoal/Black)
- **Primary Green:** `0xFF00C853` (Income text & Floating Button)
- **Danger Red:** `0xFFE53935` (Expense text)
- **Text Primary:** `0xFF000000` (Black)

##  Layout Details
- **Balance Card:** Large centered container at the top with rounded corners.
- **Balance Text:** Total amount should be large, white, and centered in the dark card.
- **Empty State:** When no data exists, display the "Note" icon (`ic_no_transactions.svg`) in the dead center of the screen.
- **Navigation Bar:** Dark background fixed at the bottom.
- **Floating Action Button:** Green circle with a white "+" placed in the bottom right corner.
